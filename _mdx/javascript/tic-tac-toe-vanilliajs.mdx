Tic-Tac-Toe is a two-player turn-based game that has its roots in ancient Egypt and dates back to roughly 1300 BC. It is played on a 3x3 grid with X and O markers. The game is won by the player who arranges their marks on the grid in a row, column, or diagonal first.

Create this in Vanilla JS.
